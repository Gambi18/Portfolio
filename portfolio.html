About Me

I grew up in an small town at the edge of the country called Bamenda, and had my Secondary education in Sacred Heart College Mankon, obtaining both his GCE O/L and A/L there. I then moved to Buea for my College Education in College Of Technology.

As a Second year student, I have experience as a Frontend Web Developer, well-versed in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and JavaScript, and studying others while aspiring to become a FullStack Web Developer.

In addition to the above, I am an amateur Artist in both Pen and Pencil Arts. I also play basketball, playing the role of a Shooting Guard with the Mid-range Record of 63%, earning the Nickname "Mid-range Meastro". I am also a music lover, and vocalist along the pitch of Alto and Tenor.
